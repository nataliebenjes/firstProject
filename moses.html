Super Heroes and Their Powers

Every hero has its powers. Here we are going to discuss every superhero and their powers. Note that there are many more others, but we will descuss only:

  • Marvel
  • DC
  1. Captain America: Enhanced strength, agility, and reflexes due to a super soldier serum, and expert in hand-to-hand combat and shield throwing.
  2. Batman: Exceptional detective skills, martial arts mastery, and an array of high-tech gadgets to fight crime.
  3. Superman: Superhuman strength, speed, and invulnerability, along with the ability to fly and shoot heat vision from his eyes.
  4. Spider-Man: Enhanced strength, agility, and reflexes, the ability to cling to walls and ceilings, and a "spider-sense" that warns him of imminent danger.
